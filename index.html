Our Services

Permanent Recruitment Executive Search HR Services Compliance

Hiring permanent employees and absorbing them in organization is a serious challenge which impacts culture, working environment and productivity of any organization. Our team focuses on understanding and Evaluating skills based on :

  • Declarative Knowledge : "what"
  • Conditional Knowledge : "why"
  • Procedural Knowledge : ”how"
  • Application Knowledge : the use of knowledge in both similar settings and in different contexts
  • Problem Solving, Critical Thinking
  • Documentation Understanding

The right leadership in an organization can scale it rapidly. Finding right senior ranking executives cab enable organizations to drive business results and achieve sustainable change. Mayiki has executive search consultants who are highly experienced professionals, serving established organisations as well as early-stage companies going through a rapid growth phase.

Working closely with the Board of Directors, CXOs, and other high-level decision-makers to understand their needs for the best leadership talent at the top management levels which will facilitate smooth execution of business strategies.

Offlate, HR outsourcing has become a dependable way for organisations to reduce operating costs, focus on core business operations and streamline internal processes. With almost 20 years of HR services experience, Mayiki have in place the necessary processes, HR consultants, technical and domain knowledge, technology, and best practices to support our clients in all aspects of human capital and employment administration.

We have experienced consultants who can help organizations in scaling activities in:

  • Payroll
  • Employee benefits
  • Trainings
  • End to end recruitment
  • HR Processes
  • HR helpdesk

For any organization to grow effectively, It is absolutely imperative to comply with all legal and statutory norms for businesses to operate successfully in India. This often involves significant investment of resources, time, and continuous monitoring to be in the know of all the changes that are happening, in order to be compliant and avoid penalties.

The organizations need a trusted partner to help them be compliant. Mayiki has the right set of people, experience and know-how to help organizations abide by government rules and regulations and are complied with without any deviation.

Our Compliance services include:

  • Payroll compliance
  • Labour Compliance
  • Provident Fund management
  • ESIC management
  • Professional tax management
  • Labor Welfare Fund Management
